GYM

I've learned that committing yourself to the
gym can be a humbling experience. At first,
it was difficult for me to maintain my hard
work ethic because I wouldn't immediately
see results. Going to the gym over the
years has taught me the importance of being
hardworking and consistent.

SPORTS

I've always enjoyed playing many sports,
ranging from golf and tennis to basketball
and volleyball. Playing sports from an
early age taught me to be strong and
competitive while being able to think
decisively on the fly. I enjoy playing as

Running a few games of basketball is also a
great way to spend time with some friends.
